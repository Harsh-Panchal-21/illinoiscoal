About Illinois Coal Association

Representing Illinois coal since 1878

The Illinois Coal Association (ICA) is a not-for-profit trade organization representing coal producers and industry partners across the state. For more than a century, ICA has served as a unified voice on legislative, regulatory, and public policy matters.

Our mission is to promote responsible coal production, protect Illinois jobs, and ensure reliable, affordable energy for communities while supporting innovation and environmental stewardship.
